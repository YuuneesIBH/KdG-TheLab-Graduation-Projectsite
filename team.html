◈ TEAM INTRO

The Arcaders is built as a shared platform project where UI, desktop orchestration, hardware integration, and security-minded tooling all meet in one cabinet experience.

This page makes the role split explicit: Younes leads platform and interface work, Rayan focuses on the hardware layer, and Matthias covers the security and lab workflows inside Guppy.

◈ HOW WE WORK
01
Platform and UI direction are handled centrally so the site, cabinet, and live demo feel like one product.
02
The hardware layer has clear ownership, so firmware, bridge setup, and physical modules stay stable during the demo.
03
The security and tooling side stays tied to a controlled lab context, so Guppy is presented in a strong but responsible way.
04
That split of responsibilities lets the team play, explain, debug, and demonstrate at the same time without losing the cabinet flow.
